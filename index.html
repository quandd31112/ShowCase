Đặng Đông Quân (Shika) · Game Designer

I'm a Software Engineering student at Van Lang University. While my major is programming, my passion is Game Design. Since my second year, I've focused on integrating these fields by using my technical expertise to develop playable prototypes that embody my design vision. My goal is to create a deep, interconnected game universe of my own. To achieve this, I'm refining my skills in Unity, turning my concepts into immersive experiences that players can engage with and understand.
Design Philosophy
I design games where mechanics tell stories. Every system I create carries meaning, turning player interaction, feedback, and decisions into a plot experience rather than relying solely on standard storytelling.
