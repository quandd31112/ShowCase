Game Designer specializing in gameplay systems and Unity prototyping. Built multiple playable prototypes exploring emotional inference, combat-driven progression, and simulation-based puzzles. Skilled in turning abstract ideas into testable mechanics and refining systems through iteration.
Design Philosophy
I design gameplay systems that rely on player interpretation and decision-making, such as emotional inference mechanics, risk-reward choices, and feedback-driven progression. My goal is to let players understand the game through interaction, not exposition.
